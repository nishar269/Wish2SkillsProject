# 🚶‍♂️ Project Walkthrough: Wish2Skill CampusOS

Welcome to the definitive guide for exploring everything Wish2Skill CampusOS has to offer. This walkthrough is designed for examiners and demo viewers.

---

## 🗝️ Phase 1: Authentication & Role Entrance
1.  **Login**: Start at `/login`. Use `admin@wish2skill.com` | `Password123`.
2.  **Dashboard**: Note the role-specific sidebar and the **Campus Scout AI** in the bottom right.
3.  **Security**: Visit `/settings` to see the profile hub and test a password change.

---

## 🏛️ Phase 2: Administrative Foundation
1.  **Onboarding**: Go to `Admin > Students` or `Admin > Faculty`. Add a new record and watch the account be created instantly.
2.  **Workload**: Go to `Admin > Workload Mapping`. Link a Faculty to a Subject. This map drives the entire logic of "who sees what" in the LMS.
3.  **Broadcast**: Post a "Global Announcement". Check the terminal/logs to see the automated **Resend Email** trigger.

---

## 📅 Phase 3: Academic Lifecycle (The LMS)
1.  **Scheduling**: As Admin/Coordinator, go to **Schedule**. Set up a class for today.
2.  **Attendance (Staff)**: Log in as Faculty. Go to **Attendance**. Click "Manage" on today's session. Mark students present/absent.
3.  **Attendance (Student)**: Log in as a Student. View the dashboard. If you're within the geofence, mark your own presence and watch your percentage bar grow!

---

## 📝 Phase 4: Examinations & Resources
1.  **Assessments**: As Faculty, create a **New MCQ Test**. Add questions and options.
2.  **Taking the Test**: As Student, go to **Assessments**. Experience the "Focused Mode" quiz interface. Submit and see your auto-graded score!
3.  **AI Insights**: Go to **Resources**. Choose a PDF material and click ✨ **Smart Info**. Read the Gemini-generated takeaway summary.

---

## 📊 Phase 5: Intelligence & Feedback
1.  **Feedback**: As any user, submit a feedback review.
2.  **Sentiment Hub**: As Authority/Admin, go to **Feedback Analytics**. See how Gemini categorized your review as POSITIVE or NEGATIVE.
3.  **Executive View**: Go to the **Authority Dashboard** to see the Recharts-powered enrollment and attendance graphs.

---

## 🤖 Phase 6: Interactive AI
-   Open the **Campus Scout** chat.
-   Ask: *"Who is the current admin?"* or *"How do I mark attendance?"*
-   Watch the role-aware AI guide you through the system.

---
**Thank you for exploring Wish2Skill CampusOS!**
